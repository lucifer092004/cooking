            <h3>Ingredients:</h3>
            <ul>
                <li>200g spaghetti</li>
                <li>100g pancetta</li>
                <li>2 large eggs</li>
                <li>50g Parmesan cheese</li>
                <li>2 cloves garlic</li>
                <li>Salt and pepper to taste</li>
            </ul>
            <h3>Instructions:</h3>
            <p>1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.</p>
            <p>2. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy.</p>
            <p>3. Beat the eggs and mix with grated Parmesan cheese.</p>
            <p>4. Combine the hot spaghetti with the pancetta and remove from heat.</p>
            <p>5. Quickly stir in the egg mixture and cook until creamy.</p>
            <p>6. Season with salt and pepper, and serve immediately.</p>
